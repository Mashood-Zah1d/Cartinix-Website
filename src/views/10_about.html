Our Mission

Why we started
Cartinix

We started Cartinix because we were frustrated — frustrated by the flood of fakes, inflated prices, and shops that treated watches as just another commodity. We believed Pakistan deserved better.

So we built something different. A store where every watch is personally verified, every price is honest, and every customer is treated like they know exactly what they're looking for — because they do.

We're not a warehouse. We're curators. And that distinction means everything.

Our Journey

How we got here

2022
The Idea

Cartinix was founded in Karachi with a single goal: make genuine luxury watches accessible to people across Pakistan without the markup or the middlemen.

2023
First 100 Customers

Word spread. Our first hundred customers became our loudest advocates. We launched on Daraz and started shipping to every major city in Pakistan.

2024
Growing the Collection

We expanded our curated catalog — adding sport, dress, and heritage pieces — while keeping our promise: nothing ships that we wouldn't wear ourselves.

2025
500+ Happy Customers

Today, Cartinix serves a growing community of watch enthusiasts across Pakistan. The collection keeps growing. The standards stay the same.
